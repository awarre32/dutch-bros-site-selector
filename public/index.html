Actionable suggestions to strengthen site selection pipeline.
High Priority

Verify VPD with NCDOT/SCDOT AADT Data

Several target sites use approximate or broker-reported traffic counts. Cross-reference every VPD figure with official NCDOT AADT portal (connect.ncdot.gov/resources/State-Mapping) and SCDOT traffic count maps before submitting LOIs.

High Priority

Engage CRE Brokers for Off-Market Pipeline

Highest-value CLT sites (Johnston Rd/I-485, Carowinds Blvd) require off-market broker outreach. Contact Cole CRE, Shook-Tarlton (CLT), NAI Southern (Matthews), CBRE/Colliers (MB coast). Build broker LOI packet with Dutch Bros brand deck + site specs.

High Priority

Prioritize Sites with Existing Drive-Thru Entitlements

Dark Arby's (Concord), former restaurant pads, and sites with explicit DT zoning save 3-6 months of entitlement timeline. These should be at the top of your LOI queue since Dutch Bros requires drive-thru by right or conditional use.

Medium

Add Demographic Heatmap Layer

Overlay Census tract-level data for median HH income, population density, and 18-34 age cohort. Dutch Bros indexes heavily on younger demographics. ESRI/ArcGIS or SimpleMaps data can be integrated into Google Maps as choropleth layers.

Medium

Track Competitor Locations (Starbucks, Dunkin, 7Brew)

Map all Starbucks, Dunkin', and 7Brew locations within 3mi of each target site. High Starbucks density validates coffee demand; 7Brew is the closest competitor for drive-thru format. This helps score Section B "competitive density" accurately.

Medium

Monitor Conway/MB SC-501 Widening Timeline

The US-501 widening from 4 to 6 lanes between Conway and Myrtle Beach will dramatically improve access to the Conway Coastal Marketplace site. Track SCDOT construction timeline - construction access impacts may affect near-term lease-up.

Low

Build Comps Database Integration

Your screener workbook has 20 NNN comps with cap rates 4.75-5.50%. Integrate this data to auto-populate exit cap assumptions by metro. Current Dutch Bros avg: Tampa 4.875%, Orlando 5.19%, GSP 5.25%. Charlotte has no DB comps yet - use coffee-adjacent 5.50-6.00%.

Low

Add Site Visit Checklist Export

Generate a printable PDF checklist for each target site covering: photo documentation, stack measurement (pace off 220ft for 16-car queue), utility vault check, adjacent business hours, morning rush observation (6-9am), and competitor drive-thru wait times.

Low

Integrate Real-Time LoopNet/Crexi Alerts

Set saved searches on LoopNet and Crexi for: "retail land for sale" in each target metro, 0.40-2.0 acres, with "drive-thru" or "outparcel" keywords. These listings turn over fast - automated alerts catch new inventory before competitors.
